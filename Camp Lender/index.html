How it Works

Super simple solution>
  1. Parent enrols camper and selects finance option
  2. Finance provider (Citizens) will take a discount (e.g. 1.7%) from the camp tuition ($4000)
  3. Finance provider pays camp the discounted balance (e.g. $3932)
  4. The Parent then repays the finance provider in equal interest free installments over the next 12 months
